# Code of Conduct

AutoXplainR is committed to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be
considerate, critique ideas and evidence rather than people, disclose relevant
conflicts of interest, and make space for participants with different levels of
experience.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxxing, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's private
information without permission.

Report concerns privately to Matteo Mazzarelli at
matteo.mazzarelli@gmail.com. Reports will be handled as confidentially and
promptly as practical. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings,
temporarily restrict participation, or permanently exclude participants when
needed to protect the community.
